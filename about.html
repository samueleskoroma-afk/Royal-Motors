Who We Are
Royal Motors Dealership and Car Rental Company Limited

Founded in 2023, Royal Motors Dealership and Car Rental Company Limited has quickly established itself as a trusted name in Sierra Leone's automotive industry. Over the past 3 years, we have proudly served over 1,400 satisfied customers, offering a wide selection of quality vehicles for sale and rent.

Located at 29A Wilkinson Road, Freetown, our showroom currently houses 30 vehicles, carefully selected to meet a variety of styles, budgets, and needs. With a dedicated team of 7 staff members (full team details coming soon), we are committed to providing personalized service, expert advice, and a seamless buying or rental experience.

Whether you're looking for your next car or a reliable rental, Royal Motors combines professionalism, trust, and excellence to ensure you drive away happy.
